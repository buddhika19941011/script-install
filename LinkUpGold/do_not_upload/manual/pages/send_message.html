Contact forms - contact.php

This script can be used by your visitors to contact you (directory owner) or owner of a link, article or blog, without the knowledge of your or link/article owner email address. The script finds the correct email in the database and sends an email with the info entered to a web form.

The link which shows the form to contact you as the directory owner is available in the top menu. The form to contact an item owner is available on the item detail pages.

These forms use the template contact_form.txt.
