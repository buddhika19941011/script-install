Tell a friend feature - tell_friend.php

This script allows your visitors to send an email to a friend about your site or any link, article, blog, video or news item in it. By default there is link to it in the top menu and also on the links, articles, blogs, videos and news detail pages. All these links open new smaller window so your visitors can email a friend without leaving the page they currently read. There are used templates tell_friend.html for the form and message_sent.html for the "email sent" page.

These variables can be used in tell_friend.txt:

#%site_url%#

URL of your site.

#%info%#

If the visitor tried to send a message to a friend, but for some reason (missing field or so) it was rejected, it shows the reason.

#%url%#

URL of the resource which will be reported. It may be URL of your site, URL of the selected item details page.

#%what%#
#%n%#

This is needed for the form to work (hidden field). Do not modify it.

#%name%#

If the visitor tried to send a message but for some reason (missing field or so) it was rejected, it shows entered name.

#%email%#

If the visitor tried to send a message but for some reason (missing field or so) it was rejected, it shows entered email.

#%friend_email%#

If the visitor tried to send a message but for some reason (missing field or so) it was rejected, it shows entered email of the friend.


Once the message has been sent, it shows a message about it in the same window.

To send the email is used template tell_friend.txt. Please note that the first line of all email templates must begin with the word "Subject: " and below the line must be one blank line. Used variables:

#%comment%#

Text of the email. This is the text taken from the form.

#%name%#

Name of the owner.
